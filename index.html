09Section

Headline insights

Three things worth raising in the meeting

May 1 → 17, 2026
⚠️ Efficiency drop

Spend doubled, ROAS fell 22%

From W18 to W20, ad spend grew +225% (₹11.2K → ₹36.5K), but ROAS slid from 4.56x → 3.54x and TACOS climbed from 9.8% → 16.9%. Sales GMV in W20 was actually −5.7% below W19 despite the much heavier investment.

🏆 Hidden winner

Branded campaigns are 6× more efficient

Branded campaigns return 26.3x ROAS on just ₹712 spend with a 12.2% CTR — vs Jam at 4.08x ROAS for ₹63.7K. Branded is starved of budget; Jam is at the point of diminishing returns.

📍 Geographic skew

Bangalore = 37% of GMV, Chennai best ROAS

Bangalore drives ₹2.07L in sales. Chennai punches above its weight: 6.4x ROAS on ₹4.8K spend (vs Hyderabad/Delhi at ~2.9x). Hyderabad has the worst spend efficiency despite being the #2 sales city.

🔻 Category drag

OB campaigns are underwater

OB (Oat Bites) campaigns spend ₹4.9K to generate ₹3.1K in ad GMV — a 0.63x ROAS. Consider pausing or restructuring before scaling further.

🍯 Product mix

Jams = 92% of revenue

Mixedberry (₹3.0L) + Strawberry (₹1.74L) + Mulberry (₹41.5K) drive nearly all sales. Honey is a distant second; PFB and OB are rounding error.

📅 Data quality

Date format and category casing need fixing

Ads dump dates were stored as MM-DD-YYYY for May 1–12 (parsed as Jan–Dec) and DD-MM-YYYY strings for May 13–17. L1 categories use Title Case in ads but lowercase in total — caused the apparent "Unknown" bucket on first pass.
